Working to promote transparency and empower citizens through data.

OpenLexington is a non-profit, non-partisan organization that is working to promote local government transparency and empower citizens through open data.

We are working with the Lexington Fayette Urban County Government, the University of Kentucky, and Code for America to make local public data more open, available and useful.

OpenLexington Volunteers are citizen engineers, professional software developers, analysts, artists, designers, students, and concerned residents. You can contribute too. Lend your talents to build something meaningful and useful. Connect with us.
